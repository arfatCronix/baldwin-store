OUR HISTORY

The History of the Company Baldwin Green Center spans with the rich heritage of Baldwin Hardware Corporation. Baldwin was born in America, and in the spirit of its birthplace, whose success is founded on vision, enterprise and hard work. Since its beginning in 1946, Baldwin has successfully aimed at setting new standards of excellence in its products for American and worldwide.

As the company grew and prospered, Baldwin began expanding its product offering into new markets and continents, and in the early 1980s Baldwin became the first brass hardware to offer homeowners the company patented. Lifetime Finish guarantee coating for its products. This set new standards of coating and durability in the decorative hardware industry. Today Baldwin's coating technology remains unique and their Lifetime for life pledge, remains free of pricing, exclusion and the risk free warranty can take on new technological finished products.

More than just a name, Baldwin identifies a birthright to claim their true talent which helps the brand gain a strong signature. These are legacy items the brand imprints into every product and the effort continues to grow stronger with each passing year. The brand's commitment to excellence in design, performance and response, as well as staying value and convenience cycle, can be traced within time keeps fine tuning its products.

In the early 1990 Baldwin entered the home decor business, starting simple and established that not a lock that has gone wrong but rather a lock that has gone right. The brand has grown to become a complete lock that has gone along perfectly. Baldwin grew from a company name to a world class brand, creating know-hows that perhaps is world's most sophisticated.
